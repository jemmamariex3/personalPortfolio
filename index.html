Career Center Mobile App

Design Concept

Most students in universities feel an overwhelming anxiety when faced with the thought of looking for an internship or a position that would provide work experiences before graduating. The pressure of having to research for opening positions is one thing, but having to prepare for the interviewing process is another. With our mobile app, students and faculties at CSUN will have a more accessible and convenient way to get all job resources that they need to help them feel more confident and ready. Developing our Career Center Mobile App allows our team to help alleviate the stress and anxiety by creating a less intimidating and fun learning environment.

Contribution

My contribution to this project are the following:

- Designed the architecture and navigational layout of the app using React Native and React Navigation

- Translated the UI/UX design into a fully functional iOS app

- Research and implemented the onboarding and splash screen features of the app

- Experimented with CSS and SVG animations for React Native

Technology Used

React-Native JS | Node JS | Xcode | Webstorm | iOS Simulator
